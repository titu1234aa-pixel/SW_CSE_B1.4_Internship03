flex-item properties:
- flex-grow [0: don't grow | 1: grow equally | 2: grow twice as much]
- flex-shrink [1: shrink if needed | 0: don't shrink]
- flex-basis [initial size before grow/shrink]
- flex shorthand [flex-grow, flex-shrink, flex-basis]
- align-self [override container alignment for one item]
- order [change visual order without changing HTML]
