Box model: tells us how every element on a web page is treated as a rectangular box. 4 important concepts(inside to outside): 1. Content: text/image/video | width x height | 2. Padding: space b/w content & border | transperant but it takes space 3. Border: Line around the padding & content 4. Margin: Space outside the border, b/w the current element & other elements
